You’re not on your own with Recollective.

Since launching in 2011, Recollective has evolved to meet the needs of every type of researcher. What began as a basic DIY software service now includes moderator training and certification courses, programming support, recruiting and more.

We continue to be a proud, participating member of the MRIA and work with QRCA and its members, many of whom are independent researchers.


You’re fully supported when you choose Recollective


school Training

Training on Recollective is complimentary for independent researchers. We deliver two sessions every week by webinar, or you can choose to watch pre-recorded sessions. Contact us to schedule your first session.


Book Training


touch_app Demo Site

Members of QRCA, MRIA or other recognized research associations are eligible for an free extended demo site. The site is provided as a test environment for you to experiment with Recollective, showcase the platform to your clients and get familiar with our latest features. Contact us to request your custom demo site.


Request Demo Site


trending_up Beyond Training

Many researchers will be satisfied with our basic training sessions and online support. But for those who want to take it to the next level, Recollective offers in-depth personalized training and certification courses. These advanced sessions are equivalent to the training we give to our specialist in-house Services team. They cover things like Activity and Task logic, Inflows Screening, Webhooks and Multi-lingual programming.
