Recruiting and Screening

There are many different approaches to finding and adding participants to an online research study. Recollective supports them all with capabilities to bulk import from spreadsheets, email direct or generate a unique study registration URL you can share.

Recollective connects via an API and webhooks to popular panel providers or customer portals and databases for continuous, seamless and highly secure participant access.


Multiple Studies

Each Recollective site can host one or more private research studies, each with separate moderators and clients which offers flexibility for structuring multi-phased research or a multi-country research program.


A Home For Your Insight Community

Drag-and-drop customizable cards to build a perfect home page for your insight community. Add rich text, photos, video, leaderboards, activity lists, highlighted discussion topics and more to the page and Recollective automatically ensures it looks perfect on every device.


Activity-based Research

Activities enable a modular approach to research. Typically structured around topics or individual days / weeks, each Activity can contain multiple Tasks to really explore a subject in depth. Participants are guided to completion and their responses can be socialized to the wider community or kept private.


Journals

Need an online diary exercise? No problem. Journals let participants respond to Tasks again and again. Completed to a set schedule or left open for ad-hoc diary entries, you can collect text, photo and video media, plus all other qual and quant response types available in Recollective using a Journal Activity.


Tasks

Tasks are the discrete questions or actions what you want participants to respond to. Recollective includes a suite of Task types that can be combined in any way into an Activity.


Real-time

Live engagement with your participants can reveal a new dimension of insight that’s not possible with asynchronous research approaches. Recollective’s Real-time Activities make it easy to engage up to 200 participants simultaneously in a text-based live chat, stimulated by prepared questions, photos, videos and polls.


Social

Activity responses and Discussion posts are displayed in a stream view to both participants and moderators. Similar to social networks, this familiar approach makes it incredibly easy to connect participants and show them the latest content, probe for more information and encourage the group to comment and dive deeper on topics.


Video

Use of video permeates Recollective. Whether you’re posting a video for participants’ review, or asking them to share a video with you, Recollective makes it intuitive and easy to upload from any device.

As moderators, you can post a video on the study home page, as part of every task or discussion topic. Participants can reply with videos to questions, share them in online diaries, or post them in response to discussion topics. Plus, every Participant video is automatically transcribed into text for excerpting and analysis.

Mobile Research

Based on a recent analysis of projects, nearly half of all research activities and engagement in Recollective happens on a mobile device. Designing for mobile couldn’t be easier on Recollective because it has an industry-leading responsive design.

That means the software is optimized for use on any smartphone and tablet mobile device. It connects to the device’s capabilities, such as cameras and media libraries, so that you have access to the full suite of platform features on mobile. Plus, as a web-app, Recollective doesn’t require any download from an app store and custom branding added to your site will also display on the mobile device.


Discussion forums

Get the power of group-based discussion, with the anytime, anywhere convenience of a bulletin board using Recollective’s Discussion forum feature. Conversations are threaded for convenience when reading, support rich media and commenting, plus you can restrict visibility to specific participant segments or a date range.


Analysis and Reporting

With so many different ways to gather deep insights, it’s essential to not only bring it all together in a intuitive, filterable way, but to display the data appropriately. Whether it’s online or exported, Recollective delivers the formats that modern researchers need.

Recollective provides Task and Activity-level data tables and charts, plus interactive word clouds, photo and video walls and a dashboard of charts. Filters apply by segment and person and the target data can be exported in customizable reports or as web, Excel and CSV transcripts.
